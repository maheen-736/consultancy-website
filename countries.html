Countries We Serve

Explore opportunities to study and immigrate in these amazing countries with ACE Consultants

United States

Home to world's top universities with excellent research facilities and diverse cultural experiences. F-1 student visas allow work opportunities during and after studies.

  • Top universities: Harvard, MIT, Stanford
  • Post-study OPT work permit available
  • Diverse range of programs
Learn More

United Kingdom

Renowned for its prestigious universities and shorter degree programs. The Graduate Route visa allows 2-3 years of work after studies for international students.

  • Top universities: Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial
  • 3-year bachelor's degrees
  • Strong research opportunities
Learn More

Canada

Known for its welcoming immigration policies and high quality of life. Post-Graduation Work Permit allows graduates to gain valuable Canadian work experience.

  • Top universities: Toronto, UBC, McGill
  • Pathway to permanent residency
  • Affordable education
Learn More

Australia

Offers world-class education in a beautiful environment with post-study work opportunities. Temporary Graduate Visa allows 2-4 years of work after graduation.

  • Top universities: Melbourne, Sydney, ANU
  • Strong focus on research
  • High quality of life
Learn More

Germany

Famous for its tuition-free or low-cost education at public universities. Excellent engineering and technical programs with strong industry connections.

  • Top universities: TUM, Heidelberg, LMU
  • 18-month post-study work visa
  • Strong economy
Learn More

New Zealand

Offers high-quality education in a safe, English-speaking environment. Post-study work visa allows graduates to work for up to 3 years after studies.

  • Top universities: Auckland, Otago
  • Beautiful natural environment
  • Welcoming to international students
Learn More

Italy

Renowned for art, architecture and fashion programs with affordable tuition fees. Rich cultural heritage and excellent food science programs.

  • Top universities: Bologna, Politecnico di Milano

  • 1-year job search visa after graduation

Finland

Known for its excellent education system and high quality of life. Offers English-taught programs with focus on technology and innovation.
